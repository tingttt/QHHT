My Experience with QHHT

The personal experiences that inspired me to walk this path.

My connection with QHHT deepened through direct personal experience. I attended a QHHT retreat, participated in a group regression, and also received a personal regression session with Jessie.

Through these experiences, I felt the power of regression in a very real way. I experienced past-life imagery, connected with the Subconscious, and found that many inner questions became clearer. I came away with a deeper feeling that life holds purpose, meaning, and guidance beyond what we always see on the surface.

That experience stayed with me and became part of why I want to offer this work to others — to create a space where people may discover insight, comfort, and something meaningful for their own journey.

What is QHHT?

Quantum Healing Hypnosis Technique (QHHT®) was developed by Dolores Cannon over decades of practice. It is described as a deep-trance hypnosis method that may include past-life regression and communication with what Dolores Cannon referred to as the “Subconscious.”

On this website, I share a simple and welcoming introduction to QHHT for those who are new, curious, or looking for a practitioner in a calm and personal setting.
