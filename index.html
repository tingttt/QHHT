My Experience with QHHT

The personal experiences that inspired me to walk this path.

My connection with QHHT became much deeper through direct personal experience. I attended the QHHT Forum, participated in a group regression with Jessie, and also experienced a personal regression session with Donale.

Through these experiences, I felt the power of regression in a very real way. I experienced past-life imagery, connected with the Subconscious, and found that many questions within me became clearer. I came away feeling that life holds purpose, meaning, and guidance beyond what we always see on the surface.

That experience stayed with me and became part of why I want to offer this work to others — to create a space where people may discover insight, comfort, and something meaningful for their own journey.
